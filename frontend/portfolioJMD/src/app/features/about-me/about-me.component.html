About me

Hi, I'm Jesús Moreno Duran, a 24-year-old software developer from Málaga, Spain, now living in Fulda, Germany. I started my journey in programming at the University of Málaga, where later I got the chance to switch things up and spend my final year at the Hochschule Fulda, which is how I ended up here.

I had the opportunity to intern at EDAG for a year and a half, a time during which I gained a deep understanding of software engineering in large-scale projects. At the end of this period, I joined the team as a software engineer, where I've had the chance to contribute and, most importantly, to learn in an environment full of interesting challenges. As Walter White once said: "I did it for me. I liked it. I was good at it." Well, I'm still working on that last part. You can find more details on this in the career section.

In summary, I've been playing around with web development for over three years, mainly using Angular and Spring Boot. Technically, I define myself as a full-stack developer, although I prefer working on the front end, probably because I enjoy designing different views and making them easy to use.
